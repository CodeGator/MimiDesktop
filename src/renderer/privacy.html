Privacy

Mimi Desktop is designed to keep your data on your device. This page describes how the application handles your information.

Data storage

All data you store in Mimi Desktop—including your master password-derived keys, logins, and notes—is kept only on your computer. It is stored in an encrypted vault file (and optional local config) in a location you can choose in Options.

Nothing is uploaded to the developer’s servers or any third-party service. No account or internet connection is required to use the app for storing and accessing your data.

No collection of personal data

The application does not collect, transmit, or share your personal data, usage analytics, or any information about the contents of your vault. There is no telemetry, no tracking, and no data sent to the cloud.

Backups and exports

If you use Backup or Export, files are created on your machine (or a location you choose). Those files remain under your control. We do not have access to them. You are responsible for securing and disposing of backups and exports appropriately.

Your responsibility

You are responsible for protecting your device, master password, and any backup or export files. If someone gains access to your computer or your master password, they may be able to access your vault. Use a strong master password and keep your system secure.

Changes to this policy

This privacy page may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in the text shown in the application. Continued use of Mimi Desktop after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
